Hi, my name is Robert Messerle and I've been developing webs for over 15 years. My primary focus has been on frontend development utilizing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript; but I've also done a good deal of backend development using Node.JS, PHP, and Ruby/Rails. Aside from programming, I am also very interested in design. I spend a good deal of time tinkering with Photography and Photoshop.
